Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience?

No! Any training will be provided. All we ask for is a smile.

When can I work?

Each day is divided into mostly 2 hours shifts so you can sign up on-line for any that you choose. We try to have two people on a shift especially at busy times.

Do I need to work the same shifts each week?

A few people do a regular shift but most people find it more convenient to pick slots week by week. There is no commitment!
